Client side: (see Client.initThread())
 - packet processor (transport thread)
 - ticks (timer)
 - chunk requestor (timer)
 - chunk prep (lighting -> building) (threads)
 - main thread
   - render -> (chunk copying)

Server side: (see Server.initThread())
 - packet processor (per client)
 - ticks (timer)
 - chunk saver (timer)
 - chunk lighter (timer)
 - chunk generator (ChunkWorker) (thread)
 - teleport worker (thread)
